About Smoke Intel

Free regulatory intelligence for the alternative products industry.

What We Do

Smoke Intel publishes free, concise briefings on regulatory changes affecting kratom, 7-OH, delta-8, THCA, nicotine vapes, and other alternative products. We track bills, enforcement actions, and scheduling orders across all 50 states so you don't have to.

Who We Serve

Shop owners, distributors, manufacturers, compliance teams, and anyone who needs to know what's legal — and what's about to change. Whether you run one store or a national distribution network, we help you stay ahead of the regulatory curve.

How It Works

Every weekday, we publish a short briefing covering the most important regulatory developments. Articles are free and always will be. For teams that need deeper coverage — full 50-state compliance data, enforcement tracking, and shop-level intelligence — we built SalesQuant.
